Humidity VS Latitude

As expected, wind speed was lower near the equator. It becomes hogher as we move away from the equator. Some outliers are recorded in northern hemisphere. More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of the year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
